This tool exists to help people quickly inspect a webpage for suspicious signs that may indicate phishing. It is intentionally simple and focuses on a few visible signals such as form behavior and submission targets.

Phishing often uses fake login pages, misleading domains, and urgent language to trick users into entering sensitive information. A lightweight inspection tool can help raise awareness before a user clicks or submits anything.
